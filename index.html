About Me

Hello! I'm Jagodage Malidu Deepsiri, a passionate web developer and graphic designer from Anuradhapura. With a strong background in HTML, CSS, Java, PHP, and UI/UX design, I specialize in creating modern, user-friendly websites and designs.

When I'm not coding or designing, I enjoy writing diaries, imagining new ideas, and spending time with my dog, Rocky.
